Wema Pos

Wema POS

Our Point-of-Sale terminals are designed to serve as alternative means for payment for goods and services, transactions can either be local or international.

Other solutions on our POS terminals include:

Customized solutions for Hospitals, Schools, Churches, Airline, and IGR collections.

  • USSD on POS
  • Pay with phone number
  • QR (Quick Response) Code
  • Contactless mode of Payments

Features

  • Payment for goods and services subject to limits set on the cards.
  • Standard MSC (Merchant Service Charge) is 0.5% per transaction capped at N1,000.

Benefits to Customers

  • Increased sales due to impulse buying by customers
  • Reduction in pilfering as cash-handling is reduced
  • Availability of multiple network technologies to ensure uptime
  • Joint merchandising opportunities
